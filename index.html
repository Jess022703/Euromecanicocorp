II · About us

A workshop built around
one marque.

The workshop · Sabana Seca
The story behind the shield

"We don't repair cars in general. We repair one type of car — and we do it well."

Euro Mecanico Corp was born from a simple conviction: the Porsche owner deserves specialized, not generalist, attention. From our workshop in Sabana Seca, every vehicle receives the same level of care we would give our own.

We use dedicated tools, OEM or certified-equivalent parts, and keep a detailed record of every service. No surprises, no sales pressure — just honest work, well documented, worthy of the shield we operate under.

EA
Edwin Avilés
Founder · Master Technician
Over 15 years focused exclusively on the Porsche platform. He personally attends every vehicle that enters the workshop.
III · Why choose us

Four non-negotiable principles.

I

Porsche only

One focus, one platform. We don't split attention between brands — all expertise is devoted to a single manufacturer.

II

If it doesn't need doing, it won't be done. We show you what was found, what's recommended, and what can wait — with evidence.

III

Quality parts
